Hello, it's me

Meysam!

About me:

I am a dynamic media professional with exposure over the last two decades across print and digital media. I have worked as a all-round video producer and documentary film maker by working on a wide range of media campaigns and projects.

I have always been committed to my personal and professional development and making my life and people around me better. Currently I am taking part in a software Development Course run by Code Your Future in London. It is a new territory for me and I am so excited to explore the challenges and the opportunities in the IT industry.

Here is my story:

1997:my first contact with HTML 3.2!

I was in my last years of high school when Iran - my country of origin - became the second country in the Middle East to be connected to the Internet.

When I became a student at Tehran University in 1997, it was the golden age of HTML3.2 code-heavy blogs of the late '90s.

I started writing a personal blog and at the time, there were not many blog templates and users had to make their own basic adjustments to the code, and it was when I was first introduced to coding!

2012: Setteling in the United Kingdom:

I started to work as a journalist in the late 1990s and early 2000s while studying at Tehran university. By the time, technology changed and I became more interested in digital media. After finishing my bachelor's degree in Business Economics in Iran, I traveled to India and studied for a Master's degree in Electronic Media (2007) and then moved to London and studied a Master's degree in International Journalism (2011).

I had to seek asylum in the UK in 2012 and I have worked as a digital journalist and TV producer since then in London.

2022: Why changing my career? Why now?

As a refugee settled in the UK, I have worked so hard to get myself and my family where I am today. Although it's not always easy to get a fair job as a refugee, I consider myself to be so lucky to have a creative job in the media industry and I believe that it's time for me to boost my career by combining my work experience in media and coding.

To cut my long story short, I think my main motivation to become a full-stack web developer is hidden in the name of the organization: CODE YOUR FUTURE! I must learn to code and change my life!
